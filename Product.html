  • Man Yee Road, Sai Kung

    麦理浩径 MacLehose Trail

    The MacLehose Trail is renowned for its spectacular natural scenery and diverse ecology, which includes a wide range of landscapes such as mountains, forests, streams and coastlines along the way. The trail is attractive to visitors who want to experience Hong Kong's countryside and natural environment.

    Nature Hiking Sea
  • 21 Ha Ling Pi, Tung Chung

    大屿山 Lantau Island

    Lantau Island, the largest island in Hong Kong, this island is known for its spectacular natural beauty, rich cultural heritage and diverse tourist activities. With the Big Buddha, a 360-degree cable car and a well-preserved fishing village, Lantau Island is perfect for relaxation.

    Mountains fishing Buddhism
  • Shek O, Hong Kong SAR

    石澳 Shek O

    Shek O is a coastal town located in southeastern Hong Kong that is known for its stunning beaches, natural beauty and rich history. Featuring one of the most popular hiking trails in Hong Kong, it is perfect for travelers looking for a short trip off the island.

    Beachhiking sea hiking
  • Cheung Chau Carpark Path

    长洲岛 Cheung Chau Island

    Cheung Chau Island is located south of Hong Kong Island, an island popular with tourists for its unique culture and traditions, beautiful beaches and tranquil countryside. Cheung Chau is a car-free island, which makes it a stark contrast to the fast pace of Hong Kong's main city.

    Island Sea Countryside
  • 3-27 Canton Road, Tsim Sha Tsui

    海港城 Harbour City

    Harbour City is one of the largest shopping centers in Hong Kong, located next to Victoria Harbour in Tsim Sha Tsui, Kowloon. It is not only a shopper's paradise, but also a gathering place for fine dining, as well as a venue for arts and cultural events with excellent views of Victoria Harbour.

    Sea luxury culture
  • 20 Tat Tung Road, Tung Chung

    东荟城 Aloe East

    Aloe East is an outlet mall located in the Islands District. This mall is famous for its large factory outlet stores offering a wide range of branded goods including clothing, sporting goods, home furnishings and electronics at prices that are usually more favorable than the retail prices in the market.

    Discounted Branded
  • 1 Matheson Street, Causeway Bay

    时代广场 Times Square

    Times Square is a large commercial complex located in Causeway Bay and is considered one of the busiest business districts in Hong Kong. Times Square boasts over 230 stores, including flagship stores of high-end international and popular fashion brands, as well as a wide range of dining options.

    fashion food
  • 500 Hennessy Road

    希慎广场 Hysan Place

    Hysan Place is a modern shopping mall located in Causeway Bay, Hong Kong. Hysan Place houses about 120 stores covering a diverse range of retail choices from internationally renowned brands to local designers. In addition, the shopping center's modern interior design creates a spacious and comfortable shopping environment.

    Tide Fashion Cafe
  • 20 Salisbury Road, Tsim Sha Tsu

    维多利亚港 Victoria Harbour

    Victoria Harbour is one of the iconic geographical features of Hong Kong. It is one of the busiest ports in the world, known for its spectacular skyline and heavy marine traffic. Hong Kong Island and the Kowloon Peninsula on either side of the harbor boast a cityscape of high-rise buildings, including many famous skyscrapers.

    Harbor skyscrapers sea
  • 128 Peak Road, The Peak

    太平山顶 Taiping Mountain

    The Peak is one of the highest points on Hong Kong Island and offers spectacular panoramic views of the city and harbor. It is particularly stunning at night, when the city lights and the starry sky intertwine to create a beautiful picture.

    bustling Hilltop
  • Lantau Island

    迪士尼 Disneyland

    Hong Kong Disneyland is a theme park located on Lantau Island, Hong Kong. As a family-friendly destination, Hong Kong Disneyland attracts not only family visitors, but also many adults who reminisce about their childhood and experience the magic of Disney.

    ParadisePlay Play
  • Mongkok, Kowloon

    旺角 Mongkok

    Mongkok is one of the busiest and most vibrant areas in Hong Kong. From trendy shopping centers such as Langham Place, it is a great place to learn about Hong Kong's local culture. The area offers a wide range of cuisines from street food to fine restaurants. It is especially known for its authentic Cantonese cuisine and snacks.

    Food Old Town
