RentRadar

RentRadar aims to simplify the renting process for first-timers to Hong Kong by providing a comprehensive online rental analysis platform. Users will be able to get a clear and intuitive view of the rental prices, location and neighborhood amenities of different apartments in Hong Kong. In addition to the basic rental services, RentRadar will also provide users with more rental information, showcasing restaurants and tourist attractions in Hong Kong for their reference. Users can also upload their needs to customize services such as travel guides, and hotel bookings.
